my top three edition

Boku no Hiro Akademia | My Hero Academia

BNHA was one of my first series that I finished and much quickly at that since I finished it within two weeks or so. It is an extremely important show to me, helping me become even just a little more confident one episode at a time. Til now I await the next chapters of the manga series as more and more crazy stuff has been happening both in the story and in real life. At our current time (January 2021) it is a few more months til season 5 comes out and I have never been more excited. Of course I can not just keep talking about this show as there is two more to write about until then do not forget to go PLUS ULTRA!!!







Kimetsu no Yaiba | Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is a rather dark turn from the shows I just finished at the time but it quickly became one of my favourites with it is amazing animation and comedic jokes in between every serious moment. As the name suggests it is quite gory but I did not find it that bad in a way, in fact I expected much worse but to my surprise it is not too horrifying (that is in the current anime, the manga is much more like what I expected). Each slayed demon would actually make you feel more and more sympathy for them as the show explains most did not have a choice and never wanted to do their mistakes. All I am saying, 10/10 and it is an emotional rollercoaster if you finished the whole thing.
